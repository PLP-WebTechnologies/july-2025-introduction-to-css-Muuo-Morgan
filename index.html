About the Park

Nairobi National Park is located just outside Kenya’s capital city. It was established in 1946 and is famous for being the only national park next to a city skyline.

Wildlife

  • Lions
  • Rhinos
  • Giraffes
  • Zebras
  • Birds

Plan Your Visit

The park is about 7 km from Nairobi city centre. Best time to visit is early morning or late afternoon for the best chance to see wildlife.
